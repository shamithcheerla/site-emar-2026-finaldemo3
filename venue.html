Arriving by Flight

The nearest airports are Rajahmundry (RJA) and Vijayawada (VGA). International delegates are advised to fly into Hyderabad (HYD) or Chennai (MAA) and take a connecting flight.

✈️ Option 1: Rajahmundry Airport (RJA)

Distance: 50 km (approx. 1 hour drive)
Recommended for delegates coming from Hyderabad, Bangalore, or Chennai. Direct flights available via IndiGo.

✈️ Option 2: Vijayawada International Airport (VGA)

Distance: 95 km (approx. 2 hours drive)
Best for international delegates or those connecting from Delhi/Mumbai.

Arriving by Train

Tadepalligudem (TDD) is a major railway station on the Howrah-Chennai main line. It is well connected to major cities like Hyderabad, Vijayawada, Visakhapatnam, Chennai, and Bangalore. The station is only 4km from the college campus.

Nearby Attractions

Explore the cultural and natural heritage around Tadepalligudem during your visit.

Dwaraka Tirumala (Chinna Tirupati)

Located 40km from Tadepalligudem, this is a famous ancient holy shrine of Lord Venkateswara. It is often referred to as 'Chinna Tirupati' (Mini Tirupati) and attracts devotees from all over South India. A perfect place for spiritual rejuvenation before or after the conference.

Papikondalu Hill Range

A scenic gorge on the Godavari river, located about 80km away. Famous for boat cruises through the winding river with majestic hills on either side. Ideal for a one-day nature trip post-conference. Overnight houseboat stays are also available.

Godavari River Front, Rajahmundry

Experience the serene beauty of the Godavari river at the famous Pushkar Ghat in Rajahmundry. The river front is especially vibrant during sunset. Just 50km from the venue, it offers a peaceful retreat with temples, boat rides, and local cuisine.
