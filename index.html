Conference Vision

Engineering materials are central to modern science and technology, enabling advances in electronics, energy storage, sensing, healthcare, and sustainable systems. Emerging trends include low-dimensional materials, multifunctional oxides, and quantum materials, with a focus on AI-driven discovery and renewable energy.
