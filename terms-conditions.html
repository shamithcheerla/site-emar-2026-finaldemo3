1. Acceptance of Terms

By accessing our website, registering for the conference, submitting a paper, or attending any conference activities, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by these Terms and Conditions, as well as our Privacy Policy.

2. Conference Registration

2.1 Registration Requirements

  • All participants must complete the official registration form with accurate and complete information
  • Registration is subject to availability and acceptance by the organizing committee
  • One registration per person is required for conference access
  • Group registrations must provide details for all participants

2.2 Registration Fees

  • Registration fees are non-refundable except as outlined in Section 4
  • Early bird discounts are valid only until the specified deadline
  • Student registrations require valid proof of enrollment
  • All fees are in Indian Rupees (INR) unless otherwise specified
  • Additional charges may apply for workshops, banquets, or special sessions

2.3 Payment Terms

  • Payment must be received in full before the registration deadline
  • Accepted payment methods include online transfer, credit/debit cards, and demand drafts
  • Confirmation will be sent upon successful payment verification
  • Incomplete payments will result in registration cancellation

3. Paper Submission and Publication

3.1 Submission Guidelines

  • All submissions must be original work not previously published elsewhere
  • Papers must follow the conference template and formatting guidelines
  • Plagiarism will result in immediate rejection and may lead to legal action
  • All authors must be listed in the submission
  • The corresponding author is responsible for all communication

3.2 Peer Review Process

  • All submissions undergo double-blind peer review
  • Review decisions are final and non-negotiable
  • Authors may be required to revise and resubmit papers
  • The organizing committee reserves the right to reject any submission

3.3 Publication Rights

  • Accepted papers will be published in conference proceedings
  • Authors retain copyright but grant the conference a license to publish and distribute
  • Selected papers may be recommended for publication in partner journals
  • Authors are responsible for obtaining permissions for copyrighted materials used in papers

3.4 Presentation Requirements

  • At least one author of an accepted paper must register and present
  • Failure to present may result in exclusion from proceedings
  • Presentations must adhere to allocated time slots
  • Presentation materials must be submitted before the deadline

4. Cancellation and Refund Policy

4.1 Participant Cancellations

  1. More than 60 days before the conference: 75% refund (25% processing fee)
  2. 30-60 days before the conference: 50% refund
  3. Less than 30 days before the conference: No refund
  4. No-show: No refund

4.2 Conference Cancellation

If the conference is cancelled or postponed due to circumstances beyond our control (force majeure), participants will have the following options:

  • Transfer registration to the rescheduled date
  • Receive a full refund of registration fees (minus transaction charges)
  • We are not liable for any travel, accommodation, or other expenses incurred

5.1 Professional Behavior

All participants are expected to:

  • Treat all attendees, speakers, and staff with respect and professionalism
  • Refrain from harassment, discrimination, or inappropriate behavior
  • Engage in constructive and respectful discourse during sessions
  • Respect intellectual property rights and confidentiality

  • Disruptive behavior during sessions or events
  • Misrepresentation of credentials or affiliation

5.3 Consequences of Violations

Violation of the code of conduct may result in:

  • Warning from the organizing committee

  • Legal action if applicable
