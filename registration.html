Abstract Guidelines

The conference invites original contributions of current or completed unpublished research work in the form of abstracts and full length papers for oral and poster presentations from esteemed Scientists, faculty from colleges, research scholars and research organizations.

The contributors are requested to send a soft copy of one page abstract in MS word format to convener.siteemar2026@sasi.ac.in with the following specifications:

  • Title: Capital letters
  • Font style and size: Times New Roman and 12
  • Authors: Font size 12 in Italic
  • Affiliations: Font size 12 in italic
  • Maximum no. of words in abstract: 250
  • Poster dimensions: 80 cm x 120 cm in vertical position

All authors must submit a signed Copyright Consent Form along with their paper submission. Please download the form below, fill in the required details, sign it, and upload it during submission.

Submission Guidelines

Format Requirements

Papers must be submitted in PDF format only (max 10MB). Please adhere to the IEEE double-column format. Length: 4 pages (no page numbers).

Plagiarism Policy

Zero-tolerance policy. Checked via Turnitin. Similarity index must be less than 20%. No self-plagiarism.

Review Process

Double-blind peer review by at least two independent reviewers. Criteria: originality, quality, relevance, and clarity.
